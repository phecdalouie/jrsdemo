About

JRS Engineering is one of the Pacific Northwest’s leading building envelope design and consulting firms. For over 15 years, we have been at the forefront of providing quality, value-conscious building science solutions. We work with architects, owners, and contractors to cultivate high-performance buildings that deliver improved durability, energy performance and value. Our building science expertise and practical understanding of construction enable us to bring technically-sound, cost-effective solutions to projects of any size and complexity.

  • 1998

    The Beginning

    In 1998, founding principal Joel R. Schwartz, P.Eng., FEC, BEP, established JRS Engineering in Vancouver, British Colombia. The company would provide specialist building envelope and roofing consulting services.

  • March 2005

    Growing Team

    In late 2005 expanded our offices to Seattle to better serve our customers wide range of needs.

  • December 2012

    Thrid Offices

    In late 2012 we developed a new team that is located in the heart of downtown Calgary. Consulting for projects like the Calgary International Airport.

  • July 2014

    Looking to the future

    Our original team of three has grown into an international firm with three offices: one in Burnaby, BC, one in Calgary, AB and one in Seattle, WA. Today’s JRS team includes experienced registered professional engineers and architects, project managers, roof inspectors, building science technologists, LEED® accredited professionals, CAD technicians and support personnel. In response to our ongoing success, we continue to grow and diversify, selecting our new team members for their superior technical knowledge, experience and desire to provide exceptional client service. Our mission is to lead our profession by consistently delivering high quality services, maintaining and cultivating our technical knowledge and valuing our client’s goals.

RUFUS 2.0

The Rufus 2.0 project, located in downtown Seattle’s Denny Triangle neighborhood, involves redeveloping three city blocks into an urban campus for a global technology company. It includes several new commercial buildings and three towers constructed in three phases.

JRS’s responsibilities include providing roofing and waterproofing design and construction review on three city blocks. Block 14 is a 37-story office tower and separate meeting facility, which is now complete. Block 19 is under construction. It includes a 37-story office tower, and sphere-shaped glass structures with intensive plantings and water features on the interior. Block 20 is currently in the design phase. JRS’s services to date have included construction documents review, bidding support, review of building envelope-related shop drawings, submittals and RFIs, and field reviews.

  • Completion Date: 2017
  • Architecht: NBBJ
  • Developer: Seneca Group
  • Location: Seattle, WA

Calgary International Airport

With Calgary’s International Airport growing and becoming a major hub for air travel in Canada, the Calgary International Airport is constructing a new International Terminal, connected to the existing terminal to service both current travel demands and new international routes.

JRS is providing below-grade waterproofing review services, as well as enhanced field review throughout construction with respect to the performance of below-grade waterproofing materials, components and assemblies. As part of our services, we are addressing the application methods, the cohesion of the material, and the adhesion of the waterproofing membrane through comprehensive quality testing.

  • Completion Date: 2015
  • Architecht: Dialog
  • Developer: Calgary Airport Authority
  • Location: Seattle, WA
