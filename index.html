RUFUS 2.0

The Rufus 2.0 project, located in downtown Seattle’s Denny Triangle neighborhood, involves redeveloping three city blocks into an urban campus for a global technology company. It includes several new commercial buildings and three towers constructed in three phases.

JRS’s responsibilities include providing roofing and waterproofing design and construction review on three city blocks. Block 14 is a 37-story office tower and separate meeting facility, which is now complete. Block 19 is under construction. It includes a 37-story office tower, and sphere-shaped glass structures with intensive plantings and water features on the interior. Block 20 is currently in the design phase. JRS’s services to date have included construction documents review, bidding support, review of building envelope-related shop drawings, submittals and RFIs, and field reviews.

  • Completion Date: 2017
  • Architecht: NBBJ
  • Developer: Seneca Group
